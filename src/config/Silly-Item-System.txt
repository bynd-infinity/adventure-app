SILLY ITEM SYSTEM
Purpose:
- Add memorable chaos without turning the whole game into a joke

Loot Tone Weights:
- Normal: 80%
- Weird: 15%
- Absurd: 5%

Rules:
- Silly items must still be mechanically useful
- Silly items may include downside, chaos, or unusual side effects
- Silly items should create story moments players remember
- Humor should be occasional, not constant

Item Tone Field:
- normal
- weird
- absurd
